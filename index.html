Mooring

Documentation woven for EFT.

The Problem

Generalist AI scribes capture words but miss the work. They don’t recognize attachment cycles, can’t distinguish primary from secondary emotion, and have no concept of Tango moves or stage progression. Notes come back generic — and you end up rewriting them.

The Approach

Mooring is being built specifically for ICEEFT-certified and EFT-trained couples clinicians. The thread metaphor isn’t decorative: documentation should reflect how the model actually moves — cycles, primary emotion underneath secondary, the progression through stages.

Built by someone with both clinical and engineering backgrounds, with the model trained on the framework you actually use.

We’re Listening First

Before building further, we’re conducting workflow interviews with practicing EFT clinicians. Thirty minutes, video call, no pitch — just questions about how you currently document and where it breaks down.

Participants get early access and shape what gets built.
